  • Treatment Plan Reviews
  • They are due every 90 days and your responsibility to keep track when they are due.
  • Sessions cannot be billed or paid if reviews are past due.
  • To complete the review...

  • How to complete certification
    1. Register for and complete the online Webnar.     The Idaho TCOM Institute now handles the CANS Certification training. Their website is https://healthandwelfare.idaho.gov/providers/behavioral-health-providers/idaho-transformational-collaborative-outcomes-management-tcom. Scroll down to the grey “Calendar” header and click on it. The Calendar will unfold to show the various trainings they offer with the times. An individual will need to register for the training they wish to attend for the CANS Certification training. Please be aware the CANS Certification Training is usually two days in a row during the specified times.

2. Request a code from the ICANS helpdesk to complete the Praed Training and get your certificate.

3. Sign an ICANS user agreement with your agency.

4. Have Agency ICANS administrator, Jim Grigg, request your login to the ICANS site.

  • Must be completed on any Medicaid client 19 years and younger.
  • Must be completed and finalized including signed off on electronically.
  • You can bill for part of them if you take more than one meeting to complete.
  • Can be done the same day as any other service.
  • Need to be completed before any regular sessions can happen.
  • Need to be completed the same time as the CDA and Treatment plan, and the same time as every review, or in other words, need to be completed with every review.
  • Need a short note to be put in the billing drawer so that Nancy knows to pay you for them.
  • Need to be done every 90 days. They expire every 90 days once expired no billing can be done on that client and sessions cannot be paid for. It is your responsibility to keep track when they expire.  (Hint: DO THEM WHEN THE REVIEWS ARE DUE!!)

  • Notes
  • Notes should be completed within 48 hours of session, however they need to completed within 7 days.
  • Leave session # blank.
  • For the “location” section either type, office or KCC if you saw the client in person, or telehealth if you saw them via video or phone.
  • Your goals on your note need to match the goals on your treatment plan.
  • In the section “treatment & diagnostic coding,” write the billing code under ICD-10code, the type of session to match the billing code, i.e. “individual” “family” etc.  AND if it was telehealth session write whether it was phone or video.  In the diagnosis box write the diagnosis code from your treatment plan.  Lastly, write in when your 90, 180, and 270 reviews are due.  This will help you keep track.
  • Notes need to be dated to the date of the note
  • Make sure to fill out the next session
  • Note must be signed to be eligible to bill.  Once you print and sign your note, place in your folder in the billing drawer.
